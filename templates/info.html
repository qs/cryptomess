What is CryptoMess

CryptoMess - is a service for keeping encrypted messages. You also can share a message to another google account, but he also need a key to decrypt it.

  • Inbox - accessed messages of other users.
  • My Messages - messages you wrote, you can edit them here.

To add access to a message fill "Access to" with user gmail. To create a private message leave "Access to" empty.

Cases of use

  1. You want to log your secret thoughts, but still keep it in secret
  2. You want to send a secret message to another person
  3. You don't want to keep all your passwords in mind. So you can use CryptoMess as password keeper with master-password
